New to the game? Here's everything you need to know to start racing, pulling stunts, and conquering the winter tracks in Moto X3M Winter.

Choose from the available winter levels. Start with the earlier stages to get comfortable with the icy physics before tackling harder courses in Moto X3M Winter.

Use the Up Arrow or W key to accelerate your bike and the Down Arrow or S key to brake or reverse. Controlling your speed is key on slippery winter surfaces.

Use the Left and Right Arrow keys (or A/D) to tilt your bike mid-air. Proper balance prevents crashes and helps you land stunts cleanly after jumps.

Performing backflips and front flips while airborne saves valuable seconds from your race time. The faster you finish, the more stars you earn!

Reach the Finish Line

Navigate through all obstacles and reach the finish flag as quickly as possible. Earn up to 3 stars per level and use them to unlock new bikes in Moto X3M Winter.
