  2. Lean & Balance: LEFT ARROW leans your rider / bike backward (essential for wheelies and backflips). RIGHT ARROW leans forward to land smoothly or dive down.
  3. Perform Stunts: Launch off massive ramps → while in mid-air, press Left or Right to spin. Complete backflips / frontflips for style and faster momentum recovery.
